About Us

The Xenox Project (Formerly Xenox Studios) was founded in late 2020 by Xenox & Alfish9133.
Our Goal was to make engaging, fun indie games, and to use these games as a learning experience, to expand our knowledge
on game development. Our current projects, Grovely Woods and Ventura have both been in development for some time now,
with release dates for both games expected late 2025/early 2026.
